A Box of Agile

Just a round-up of the stationery I normally buy to help in professional contexts. It’s useful to have it all in the same list.

  • Sticky Notes: No good technology team can do their job without them.
  • Markers: The companion to your sticky notes.
  • Pens: The right pen can make all the difference.
  • Notebooks: With every pen you need paper.
  • Wall Postits: Cocreate in a meeting room.
  • Storage: A place to keep it all
